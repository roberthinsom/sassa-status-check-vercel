How to Resolve 'Approved but No Payment Date' for Your SASSA SRD Grant

What Does 'Approved but No Payment Date' Mean?

When you perform a SASSA status check online or via USSD, you may see the message "Approved but No Payment Date." This means that SASSA has reviewed your application, and you have been approved to receive the SRD grant. However, a payment date has not been assigned to you yet.

Several factors can contribute to this delay, including system backlogs, missing banking details, or verification delays. Understanding these factors can help you take the necessary steps to resolve the issue.

Common Causes of 'Approved but No Payment Date'

  • Payment Cycles and Delays: The SRD grant payments are processed in monthly cycles. High demand or technical issues may cause delays in assigning a payment date.
  • Incorrect or Missing Banking Details: If your banking information is incorrect or not updated, SASSA won't be able to process your payment.
  • Verification and Validation Issues: SASSA uses multiple systems to verify applicants' details, and delays in this process can affect payment scheduling.
  • High Volume of Applications: A backlog due to a high volume of applications can cause delays in processing and assigning payment dates.
  • Eligibility Rechecks: Sometimes, SASSA needs to verify certain details about your eligibility before confirming a payment date.

How to Resolve 'Approved but No Payment Date'

If you’re seeing "Approved but No Payment Date" on your SASSA status check, here are the steps you can follow:

1. Check Your Banking Details

One of the most common reasons for delayed payments is incorrect or missing banking information. To resolve this:

  • Log in to the SASSA SRD portal: Visit the official SASSA SRD Portal and log into your account.
  • Verify your banking information: Ensure that your bank account details are correct.
  • Update your information: If there are any errors or outdated details, update them immediately to avoid further delays.
  • Consider alternative payment methods: If you don’t have a bank account, update your payment method to receive your payment via retail partners or post offices.

2. Perform a SASSA Status Check

Visit the SASSA SRD Portal or dial *134*7737# on your mobile phone to check the status of your application. This will provide you with detailed information on the progress of your payment.

3. Verify Your Eligibility

Ensure that you meet the eligibility criteria for the SRD grant. If your financial situation has changed or there is new information that SASSA needs to verify, updating your details can speed up the process.

4. Be Patient and Stay Informed

Payment cycles may vary, and delays due to system backlogs are common. Continue to monitor your status and check for updates from SASSA.

5. Contact SASSA for Assistance

If the issue persists, contact SASSA for further assistance:

  • Call the SASSA helpline at 0800 60 10 11.
  • Send a message to SASSA’s official WhatsApp number: 082 046 8553.
  • Visit your nearest SASSA office with your ID and other supporting documents for help.

6. Consider Alternative Payment Methods

If you are still facing difficulties with bank payments, you can opt for alternative methods such as collecting your grant at the post office or at participating retail stores like Shoprite or Checkers.

Conclusion

Seeing "Approved but No Payment Date" on your SASSA status check is not uncommon, but by following the steps outlined above, you can resolve the issue and receive your SRD grant. Keep your details updated, check your status regularly, and stay patient. If needed, contact SASSA for assistance to ensure that your payment is processed smoothly.
